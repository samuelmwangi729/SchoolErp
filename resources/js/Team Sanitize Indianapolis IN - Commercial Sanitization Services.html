HOW DO WE SANITIZE vIRUSes?

Our aim is to provide reliable and affordable sanitizing & disinfecting services. According to the CDC Recommendations cleaning of visibly dirty surfaces followed by disinfection is a best practice measure for prevention of viruses. One way we do this is through electrostatic cleaning. Electrostatic disinfection is one of the best techniques available to sanitize or disinfect viruses from offices, buildings, and medical facilities. We spray electrostatic mist on all surfaces including the places that have always been hard to reach with conventional methods. Electrostatic mist is an excellent way to ensure thorough and concise sanitization of all surfaces.
